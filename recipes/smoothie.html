My best Smoothie

This is best smoothie so far. I created this one on a very hot day in June. I was thirsty as hell and I needed something juicy, fresh and flavouful. I looke in my fridge and voil√†. The Eureka moment. Here's my recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 Banana
  • 2 Tbsp Peanut Butter
  • 200ml Milk of choice
  • 2 Ice cubes

Steps

  1. Peel your banana, but don't put it in the wrong hole
  2. Put all the ingredients in a powerful blender (the banana too) and mix until smooth
  3. Pour the smoothie in a tall Glass, decorate with some toasted peanut, chooped
  4. Drink it all and enjoy!
