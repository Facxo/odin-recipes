Faxo's Mango Cheescake

This is the best cheesecake I've ever made. Fresh and juicy. The mango gel√® is super!

Ingredients (8 people)

For the base
  • 240g Digestive biscuits
  • 120g Melted butter
For the cream
  • 400g Cream Cheese
  • 150g Greek Yogurt, plain
  • 250g Heavy cream
  • 100g Sugar
  • 10g Gelatin
  • 6 Mint leaves
  • 1 Tbps Lemon Juice
For the topping
  • 1 Mango
  • 4g Gelatin

Steps

  1. Put the Buscuits in a blender and mix it to get a sand texture, then add the melted butter, mix well and place the mix in a cake stamp and with a tablespoon make it even and pressed. Place in the fridge
  2. Take the cheese, the yougurt and the sugar and mix well, add the mint leaves (chopped).
  3. Whip 230g of cream and add to the cheeses
  4. Place the galatin in cold water. Put 20g heavy cream in a pan and bring it to simmer, remove from heat and add the squeezed gelatin. Let it cool for 2min and add it to the mixture
  5. Now add the cream over the buscuit base and place in the fridge for a couple of hours
  6. Now peel and cut the mango into pieces. Blend smoothly and pass it through a colin. Take 50g of mango sauce e place in a pan. Bring it to simmer, add the gelatin like the cream and mix it back with the other mango sauce. Now pour it over the cheese cream and let it refrigerate for another couple of hours
  7. Remove the basil, add a splash of olive oil and serve.
  8. Decorate the cheescake as you wish and eat it!
